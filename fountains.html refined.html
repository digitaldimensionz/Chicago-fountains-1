Why This Site Exists

This project maps public drinking water fountains around the Chicago area. It helps runners, cyclists, tourists, and everyday residents find clean water while exploring the city.

Features

  • Interactive Google Map with clickable fountain markers
  • Searchable fountain list
  • Dark/Light mode toggle
  • Photo gallery
  • Mobile-friendly layout

Future Plans

More fountains will be added, along with user-submitted locations, accessibility info, and seasonal availability.
